    #. Editar o *Name* do arquivo de ***.txt** para ***.csv** > **Save** > **Yes** > **Save**:

            * Line termination: Unix (linefeed)
            * Separator: Semicolon (;)
            * Quoting: Always
            * Quote character: "
            * Character encoding: Unicode (UTF-8)
