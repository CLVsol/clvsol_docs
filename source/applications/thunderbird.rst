Thunderbird
-----------

	* `Thunderbird <https://www.thunderbird.net>`_
		Thunderbird is a free email application that’s easy to set up and customize - and it’s loaded with great features!

Add-ons 
-------

	* `Extensions <http://kb.mozillazine.org/Extensions_%28Thunderbird%29>`_ 
		Extensions are small adds-ons that give new functionality to complement Thunderbird's built-in features, so that you can customize Thunderbird to fit your own individual needs. 

	* `Lightning <https://addons.thunderbird.net/thunderbird/addon/lightning/>`_ 
		Organize your schedule and life's important events in a calendar that's fully integrated with your Thunderbird email. Manage multiple calendars, create your daily to do list, invite friends to events, and subscribe to public calendars.

	* `gContactSync <https://addons.thunderbird.net/thunderbird/addon/gcontactsync/>`_ 
		Synchronizes contacts & groups between Thunderbird and Google Contacts.

	* `Contact Tabs <https://addons.thunderbird.net/en-US/thunderbird/addon/contact-tabs/>`_ 
		Contact Tabs enhances Thunderbird to give you quick access to all your contacts directly from Thunderbird's main window: Use the powerful search field to find your contacts and open them in a new tab for viewing and editing.

	* `Quick Folder Move <https://addons.thunderbird.net/thunderbird/addon/quick-folder-move/>`_ 
		When filing messages, a search box will show up allowing you to quickly enter the folder name you want to move to.
		If no search term is entered, a list of recent folders will show up instead.

	* `ImportExportTools <https://addons.thunderbird.net/thunderbird/addon/importexporttools/>`_ 
		Adds some tools to import and export folders and messages.

		Mozilla Thunderbird is the safe, fast email client that's easy to use. It has lots of great features including quick message search, customizable views, support for IMAP/POP, RSS support and more. Plus, the portable version leaves no personal information behind on the machine you run it on, so you can take your email and adress book with you wherever you go.
